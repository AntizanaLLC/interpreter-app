# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 16, 2026

Antizana LLC ("we", "our", or "us") built **Interpreter - Voice Translator** as a commercial app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

---

## Information We Collect

### What stays on your device
- **Audio recordings** — captured by the microphone and processed entirely on-device by Whisper AI. Audio is never uploaded to any server.
- **Translation history** — stored locally on your device. We have no access to it.

### What may leave your device
- **Transcribed text** — when Apple's on-device Translation framework is unavailable (iOS 17–25) or times out, the transcribed text may be sent to [MyMemory](https://mymemory.translated.net) (a third-party translation API) to complete the translation. This is sent anonymously with no user identifier attached.

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- We do not require account creation or login
- We do not track you across apps or websites
- We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs in this app

---

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data sent |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| MyMemory API (`api.mymemory.translated.net`) | Fallback translation when on-device translation is unavailable | Transcribed text only, anonymously |
| Apple Translation Framework | On-device translation (iOS 26+) | Processed entirely on-device |

---

## Data Retention

All data (translation history, downloaded models) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time from the app's Settings screen or by uninstalling the app.

---

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
